About Zero Day Club

"Pray for the Best, Prepare for the Worst and Expect the Unexpected"

The Zero Day Club is a cyber-security club at Punjabi University, Patiala. Our motive is to teach students and aware them about hacking and its applicaions also how to overcome being a victim of a cyber attack. We thrive to provide opportunities to learn & access deep technical content by hosting cool training workshops, collaborating with amazing community members, organizing tech talks and various conferences.

Things we Teach

Cyber Security

Cybersecurity or information technology security is the protection of computer systems and networks from information disclosure,theft of or damage to their hardware,software,or electronic data and disruption or misdirection of the services they provide.

Linux

Linux is a family of open-source Unix-like operating systems based on the Linux kernel, an operating system kernel first released on September 17, 1991, by Linus Torvalds. Linux is typically packaged in a Linux distribution

Networking

Networking is the practice of transporting and exchanging data between nodes over a shared medium in an information system. Networking comprises not only the design, construction and use of a network, but also the management, maintenance and operation of the network infrastructure, software and policies.

Python

Python is an interpreted high-level general-purpose programming language. Its design philosophy emphasizes code readability with its use of significant indentation. Its language constructs as well as its object-oriented approach aim to help programmers write clear, logical code for small and large-scale projects
